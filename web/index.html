01 / Thesis

Not all participation is equal.

A launch with 100 wallets that dump quickly is not healthier than a launch with 30 real participants who add liquidity, hold, return, and stabilize the market. CoordiFlow makes that difference visible and programmable â€” transforming a token launch from "who bought the most" into "is this market forming through healthy coordination."

  • Coordination measures market formation.

A hook that watches the market form, then shapes it.

Step 03

Step 04
