01 / Thesis

Not all participation is equal.

A launch with 100 wallets that dump quickly is not healthier than a launch with 30 real participants who add liquidity, hold, return, and stabilize the market. CoordiFlow makes that difference visible and programmable — transforming a token launch from "who bought the most" into "is this market forming through healthy coordination."

  • Volume measures noise.
  • Wallets measure breadth.
  • Coordination measures market formation.
02 / Mechanism

A hook that watches the market form, then shapes it.

  1. Step 01

    Attach

    Install at the Uniswap v4 Hook layer of any compatible pool, including Flap-style launches on X Layer.

  2. Step 02

    Observe

    Every swap, mint, and burn updates wallet state on-chain. No oracles, no off-chain trust assumptions.

  3. Step 03

    Classify

    Wallets earn a persona — Seeder, Builder, Stabilizer, or Restricted — based on their actual flow profile.

  4. Step 04

    Respond

    Fees, caps, stages, liquidity unlocks, and reward eligibility shift with coordination quality.

03 / Personas

Real-time behavior classification.

Persona assignment is deterministic from observed actions and updates with every interaction. Wallets move between personas as their behavior evolves — no committee, no off-chain scoring service.
